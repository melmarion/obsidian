### **Song Blueprint Structure**

**1. TRACK IDENTITY**

- **Working Title:** (Must feel like an archive code or a cryptic scene caption. e.g., "404_HEARTBREAK.rar", "CRYSTALSPLITTER", "DEATHWISH_FASTLANE")
    
- **Core Vibe in One Sentence:** (e.g., "The sound of stealing your ex's luxury watch and driving to a server farm at 3 AM.")
    
- **Target Platform for Virality:** (e.g., "TikTok (focused on 'niche' and 'vibe' discovery), SoundCloud (for the raw upload), curated Spotify playlists with names like 'hexed & online'")
    

**2. SONIC ARCHITECTURE (The "How it Sounds")**

- **BPM & Key:** 68-75 BPM, minor key (A-minor, F#-minor). Slow enough to feel narcotic, with space for the vocal to drag.
    
- **Beat Breakdown:**
    
    - **Drums:** **Tay-K inspired** – hard, distorted 808 kick with a long tail, crisp but sparse trap snares (hitting on the 3), skittering, panning hi-hats for motion. The kick must **punish** the mix.
        
    - **Bass:** A **wobbly, detuned synth bass** that follows the 808 pattern but with slight portamento (glide) to create a "sick" feeling.
        
    - **Pads/Atmosphere:** **Cloud Rap / "New Age" core** – ethereal, reverb-drenched synth pads that sound like a corrupted Windows startup sound or a slowed-down Gregorian chant sample. Lots of air, hiss, and vinyl crackle texture in the high end.
        
    - **Melodic Elements:** A single, mournful, looped melody played on a **music box or glockenspiel synth** that's been bit-crushed and drenched in delay. This is the "Lana" touch—beautiful but decaying.
        

**3. VOCAL & LYRIC BLUEPRINT (The "How it Feels")**

- **Delivery Style:** A fusion of:
    
    - **Lana Del Rey:** Slurred, breathy, melancholic lower register, almost spoken-sung. A sense of doomed, cinematic romance.
        
    - **Tay-K:** Sudden, blunt, aggressive barks on certain lines, a confrontational and nihilistic edge. A "don't-give-a-fuck" monotone that flips into menace.
        
- **Lyrical Content & Themes (MUST INCLUDE):**
    
    - **"Hexxed Webcore" Imagery:** References to corrupted files (.gif tears), obsolete tech (CRT glow, VHS static), digital paranoia ("they watching thru the cam").
        
    - **High/Low Tension:** Juxtapose **luxury/designer** mentions (Margiela, chrome hearts, dark wine) with **street/grit** (county jail calls, stolen cars, "counting up in the parking lot").
        
    - **Detached Hedonism:** Sex, drugs, and violence described with bored, poetic disaffection. "We did lines off the iPhone box / You cried, I checked the crypto stocks."
        
    - **Mantra Hook:** A simple, repeatable 4-6 word hook that sounds like a **TikTok bio or IG caption**. (e.g., "Goth money, sunset dread," "Live fast, die glitched.")
        
- **Song Structure Suggestion:**
    
    - **Intro:** (0:00-0:30) Atmosphere pad + music box melody fades in.
        
    - **Verse 1:** (0:30-1:15) Lana-style delivery, establishing scene and internal monologue.
        
    - **Chorus/Hook:** (1:15-1:45) Beat drops fully. Mantra hook repeated 4x, vocals layered and slightly distorted.
        
    - **Verse 2:** (1:45-2:30) Tay-K energy increases. More aggressive, confrontational bars.
        
    - **Bridge:** (2:30-3:00) Beat strips back to pads and kick. A spoken, confessional Lana-style moment.
        
    - **Outro:** (3:00-3:30) Hook repeats, vocals become more processed and fade into the atmospheric noise, ending with a sound of a dial-up tone disconnecting.
        

**4. VISUAL & MARKETING SEED (The "How it's Seen")**

- **Cover Art Concept:** A glitched, VHS-filtered still life: a wilted rose on a dusty CRT monitor displaying a low-polygon heart. A designer bracelet sits next to a cracked iPhone. Text in a distorted, archaic computer font.
    
- **Initial Snippets for TikTok:** Identify two 5-7 second clips:
    
    1. The **"Aesthetic" snippet:** The bridge's most melancholic, pretty vocal line over the music box melody. For "mood" edits.
        
    2. The **"Niche Flex" snippet:** The most aggressive Tay-K-style bar dropping right as the 808 hits. For "rep the set" or fit-check videos.
        
- **Target Collaborator for a Remix/Feature:** An artist like **Destroy Lonely** or **Ecco2k**—someone who exists in the overlap of cloud rap, niche fashion, and online aesthetics.
    

**Final Output:** Present the complete **Song Blueprint** using the four sections above. Be sonically and lyrically specific. This is a production brief, not a vague idea.
