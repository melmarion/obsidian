**Processing Chain (The "Flattening" Protocol):**

**1. ISOLATION & PREP:**
*   Strip the sample of its original context. Remove any low-end thump, reverb, or orchestral backing using EQ and spectral editing. We want the **naked vocal timbre**.

**2. PITCH & FORMANT SHIFT (The Ghosting):**
*   **Pitch Shift:** Increase the pitch by **+5 to +12 semitones**. Target the range where the voice becomes strained, ethereal, and slightly unnatural—not cute, but piercing.
*   **Formant Shift:** Experiment with *slightly raising the formants* to further enhance the nasal, forward quality. Avoid the "chipmunk" effect; we want "alien siren."

**3. QUANTIZATION & GRID-LOCK (The "Flatness"):**
*   Slice the sample to the nearest **1/16th note**.
*   Apply **100% quantization** to a strict grid. **Remove all swing.** The goal is to have the organic vocal line **conform rigidly to the electronic pulse**, creating friction.

**4. FILTERING & TEXTURING (The Burial):**
*   **EQ:** Apply a high-pass filter around **300Hz** to remove body. Boost sharply between **2kHz and 5kHz** to accentuate the nasal "bite." Cut boxy mids around 800Hz.
*   **Compression:** Use aggressive compression (4:1 ratio or higher) to **flatten the dynamic range** and make it sit like a synth pad, not a performance.
*   **Saturation:** Add subtle tape or tube saturation to warm the extreme highs slightly and prevent a sterile digital sound.

**5. SPATIAL TREATMENT (The Haze):**
*   **Reverb:** Use a **short, bright plate reverb** (decay time: 0.8s - 1.2s). Pre-delay: 5-10ms. The goal is a **halo, not a space**. It should feel close yet evaporated.
*   **Delay:** Add a **single, rhythmic slap delay** (1/16th or 1/8th note) with a low feedback (1-2 repeats). This creates the hypnotic, echoing effect without clutter.

**6. ARRANGEMENT & INTEGRATION:**
*   **Loop it as a Mantra:** The processed sample should be looped **ostinato** throughout a section of the song (verse or chorus).
*   **Frequency Pocket:** Carve out a space in your mix (around 2k-5k) for this element to dominate. Side-chain pad synths slightly to it so it cuts through without increasing volume.
*   **Function:** It is not the lead. It is a **hypnotic textural bed** that provides the "coded" emotional signature. It should feel like a memory of a song playing from another room in a dream.
