### **PROJECT: HIVE**

**Tagline:** *Your Attention is the Resource.*

**Core Premise:** The “game” is merely the public-facing ritual. The true product is a **real-time, quantifiable social hierarchy** that you *live inside*. It’s less like Candy Crush and more like a **silent, permanent stock market for social capital**, where your portfolio is your attention.

#### **1. The Core Mechanic: The “Pulse”**
*   You open the app. You see a **live, minimalist feed** of actions from people in your “Hive” (initially seeded from contacts, then algorithmically expanded).
*   Each action is a **Pulse**: a song someone listened to, a link they saved, a photo they took, a phrase they typed. No context. Just raw, timestamped data: `Kai listened to [Destroy Lonely - BANE]`. `Sloane saved [article on neural lace]`.
*   **Your only action:** You can **“Attune”** to a Pulse (a single tap). This doesn’t “like” it. It means: *“I register this signal.”*
*   **The Addiction Hook:** The feed never ends. It’s a live stream of the cerebral lives of your most culturally relevant peers. Not checking it means **missing signals**, falling out of sync, losing social positioning. It’s FOMO weaponized into a direct IV drip.

#### **2. The Addictive Architecture: The Social Ledger**
This is where it becomes a drug. Every interaction builds your **Social Calculus**.
*   **Influence Score:** A hidden number. You gain points when others **Attune** to *your* Pulses. You lose points slowly over time. This score is never shown directly, but it determines your **visibility and reach** in the Hive.
*   **The Sync Map:** A private, evolving network graph that shows you, in near-real time, **who is attuning to the same Pulses as you**. It creates invisible tribes. You see a connection form between two acquaintances **before they do**. You possess secret social knowledge.
*   **The Aura:** Based on your Pulse history and Influence Score, you are assigned a silent, changeable **Aura** (e.g., `SUBSTRATUM-CORE`, `GHOSTEDGE`, `PRIMERED`). This is your **social phenotype**. It is displayed next to your name. People will start to tailor their own Pulses to **shift their Aura**, manufacturing their output to game the classification.

#### **3. The “Game” Layer: Curated Scarcity**
Once per day, the system **curates a single “Artifact.”**
*   It could be: an unreleased song snippet, a password-protected link to an essay, a high-res image from a secret photoshoot.
*   **Access is tiered by Influence Score.**
*   The Artifact is only available for **24 hours**. After that, it vanishes forever. It is never discussed in the open feed; you can only **Pulse your Attunement** to it.
*   **This creates a daily mandatory check-in.** Missing a day means missing a unique, status-defining piece of cultural data. Possessing it first is a silent victory.

#### **4. The Monetization: Paying for Opacity & Intel**
*   **Free Tier:** Live Pulse feed. Basic Aura. Can Attune.
*   **Subscription Tier (“The Hive Mind”):**
    1.  **See the Influence Scores** of others.
    2.  **Access the full, unfiltered Sync Map.**
    3.  **Get alerts** when your Aura is about to change, or when a rival’s Score passes yours.
    4.  **View the “Pulse History”** of any user (what they’ve been attuning to for the last 7 days).
*   You are not paying for features. You are paying for **the social chessboard and the pieces’ hidden stats**. You are buying paranoia and advantage.

#### **5. The Endgame: Becoming the Signal**
The highest tier isn’t purchased. It’s **bestowed**.
*   If your Influence Score and Aura reach a clandestine threshold, you become a **“Source.”**
*   Your Pulses are broadcast to wider segments of the Hive.
*   You gain the ability to **seed one Artifact per month** to the entire network.
*   You are now part of the system’s curation engine. Your addiction is rewarded with **a hit of pure, god-like cultural influence**. You are no longer a player; you are a component of the drug you’re hooked on.
