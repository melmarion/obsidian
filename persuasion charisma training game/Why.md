How this helps you read people:

Ask: "What need is this person trying to meet right now?"

- Someone being aggressive → probably trying to meet safety/control needs
- Someone name-dropping → trying to meet significance needs
- Someone asking questions like you just did → trying to meet understanding/connection needs while avoiding vulnerability

- Universal human needs (roughly in order):

1. Safety/survival
2. Belonging/connection
3. Significance/mattering
4. Autonomy/control
5. Growth/meaning

- Charismatic people identify what someone needs (connection, significance, understanding, validation) and give it to them genuinely in the moment.
- That's the core of charisma - making people feel seen in the specific way they want to be seen.
