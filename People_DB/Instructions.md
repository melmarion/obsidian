### **The Obsidian Power User Setup for People Profiling**  
*(Maximize efficiency, minimize friction)*  
🧠 Templates
- `_Templates/` stores your profiling template (more on this below).  

*(Install Dataview via Obsidian’s Community Plugins if you haven’t already.)*  
#### **Weekly Review:**  
1. Search `#people/manipulator` to see who needs neutralizing.  
2. Check `file.mtime` to spot neglected profiles.  
#### **A. Embed Audio Snippets**  
- Record covert voice memos during conversations (e.g., with Otter.ai).  
- Drag the file into their profile note.  
- **Why?** Tone analysis reveals hidden tells.  
#### **B. Graph View Warfare**  
- Enable **local graph** for a person.  
- See who they’re linked to (allies/rivals).  
- **Example:** If Emily is tied to 3 adversaries, she’s likely a gossip hub.  
#### **C. Mobile Syncing (No Subscription)**  
- Sync via **Syncthing** (free, encrypted) or **Dropbox**.  
- **Why?** Log interactions in real-time (e.g., after a dinner).  
**Remember:**  
*"A 70% profile updated daily beats a ‘perfect’ one that never exists."*
