## My first private jet experience:

After not being friends with Naomi for so many years, i seemed to gain her respect through my snappy remarks. I started defending myself and its almost as if i had finally proven my worth. I will bully someone if they bully me because they deserve it. 

After Naomi saw my new found worth, she decided to invite me to her father's private het for a girls trip with another friend. She was kind and pretty with blond hair and a green long-sleeve.

We decided on a good place but we were on a time crunch because we just got out of class and didn't want to come home too late. The place that we agreed on was coos bay. A thirty min fight compared to the inconvenient 3 hrs.

The strangest thing about this experience was the seating. 

It was just us three girls and only the staff. Naomi sat in the back and us two were in the front but opposite ends. Her left, me right. We could see a nice view but it was mostly about the comfort that made me so happy. I am finally that girl worthy of this lifestyle. I am content, i am happy.

Later Naomi joined us to the front and we made small talk but not in a fake way. It feels good to be real. I feel most at home with the higher class.

After our trip ended, I actually met her dad, who owned a tech company. He invited me for dinner that night and the preceding nights. I guess i left an excellent impression due to me leaving room for mystery.
