"Reverse Cramer" refers to the investment strategy of taking the opposite position of recommendations made by CNBC's Jim Cramer, popularized by the [Inverse Cramer Tracker ETF (SJIM)](https://finance.yahoo.com/quote/SJIM/performance/) and social media trends. Data suggests that betting against Cramer's picks has historically outperformed following them. While [SJIM](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/inverse-cramer-wins-again-opposite-003304004.html) was closed in February 2024, the concept remains a popular, often meme-driven, approach to market speculation.
