This initial meeting usually takes around 20 minutes, and it's a chance for us to establish a strong foundation of trust and understanding. I'll be able to learn all about your pets' unique quirks and preferences, and you'll have the chance to see firsthand the level of care and attention I provide. *Update: I am currently caring for a multi-month petsitting with a long time family friend in Oregon.
